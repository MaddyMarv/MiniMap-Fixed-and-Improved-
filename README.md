### [Minimap Fixed and Improved](https://www.nexusmods.com/warhammer40kdarktide/mods/633)
**The short version:** Adds a functional and customizable minimap to your HUD.

**The details:** Standalone mod that gives you an actual working minimap on your screen. It is fully customizable so you can tweak the size, zoom level, and opacity. It tracks teammates and objectives so it makes it a lot easier to stay grouped up during fights and not get lost.
